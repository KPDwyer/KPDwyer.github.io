About

I'm a Senior developer in Toronto, Ontario. I'm Exploring as much of everything as I can in my free time, while helping my kiddos do the same. My previous Jam games have included VR, NFC and alternative inputs. These days I'm experimenting with Godot, Krita & Blender.
 
I mostly post on Mastodon, though less than I'd like.
 

I use Github a lot, but mostly in private repos.

 

My Itch account is usually up to date with whatever I've been working on.
